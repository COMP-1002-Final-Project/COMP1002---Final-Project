About Outpost

Manage your resources, construct your factories, and build your outpost.

Outpost is a board game wherein you compete with other players to build the best outpost that you can. Published between 1991 to 1994 by TimJim Games, the game is intended for three to eight players, has an estimated playtime of between one and a half to three hours, and is recommended for ages 12+.

  • Build and staff your factories
  • Produce resources
  • Improve your factories
  • Compete in auctions
  • Purchase factory improvements

The game has three phrases, with different improvements available during different phases. Improvements are purchased through auctions, and each improvement is worth points. Each improvement offers unique advantages, such as improved technologies, better options for storing resources, increased population limits for resource-producing factories, and so forth. Each staffed factory is also worth points. The player who reaches 75 points first wins.
