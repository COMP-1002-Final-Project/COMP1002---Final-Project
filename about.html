About Outpost

Manage your resources, construct your factories, and build your outpost.

Outpost is a board game wherein you compete with other players to build the best outpost that you can. Published between 1991 to 1994 by TimJim Games, the game is intended for 3-8 players, and has an estimated playtime of between one and a half to three hours.
